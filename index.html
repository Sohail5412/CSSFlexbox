Communication

Cats have a specialized communication system. They communicate using a variety of vocalizations (meowing, purring, hissing) as well as body language.

Group Names

A group of cats is called a clowder. You can also refer to it as a 'glaring' of cats. A male cat is called a 'tom', a female cat is called a 'molly' or 'queen', while young cats are called 'kittens'.

Unique Features

Cats have more bones in their bodies than humans. Cats don’t have a sweet tooth. Every cat’s nose pad is unique, much like a human fingerprint.
